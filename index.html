Astronomical society of RIT (ASRIT),an imprint of RIT ISTE chapter,funtioning under a set of constitutional bylaw is a platform for the amateur astronomers of Rajiv Gandhi Institue of Technology , kottayam who have ever took time to be an overwhelming stargazer cum space lover,providing an ocean of opportunities and hopes to flee through the Galaxy of wonders. Its a dynamic space where you find like-minded astronomy enthusiasts thirsty for your bit of knowledge and hungry for your piece of ideas,together write an an irreplacable page in history.Developed by an ambitious team from RIT,ASRIT is here to quench their thirst and to make a fascinating breakthrough into the universe. The motive behind such an organisation is not only educational,but in nourishing passion and interest in astronomy among the upcoming generations,involving them in innumerable club activities. This campus community with students and the professors to guide you,attempt to bring forward the space-lovers of RIT and contribute our piece of knowledge to the society. Its the first step towards developing our own Newtonian Telescope during the time race.

VISION

To organize the astronomy enthusiasts and Popularize the role of engineers in space science.

MISSION

To enhance the knowledge and interest of astronomy enthusiasts, to connect engineering disciplines with space technology and ultimately to be an active element in inscribing history by fostering our efforts from theory to practical aspects.

PURPOSE

  1. Nurture scientific temparement among its members and the community as a whole.
  2. Encourage its members to reach out to the scientific community and organisations in India or abroad.
  3. Encourage contributions to the field of astronomy from engineering disciplines.
  4. Organize events,viz. seminars,workshops, hands-on sessions, quizzes,etc. to the field of astronomy and engineering.

WHAT WE DO

Being an ASRIT member, we have in store for you a handful of resources and opportunities to showcase your golden ideas and to proffer the best to the community.

Events

Quiztronomy

Its an inter-college Quiz competition held within the RIT campus,with competitors from different zones to scale up your knowlegde about the heaven you are part of. With an effective judging panel of experts and scientists,its a lucky chance for you to lock horns with the best.

Seminar and Courses

We offer you with brainstorming seminar classes led by well-known and efficient personalities from reputed institutions to enlighten your thoughts with the new breakthroughs and space discoveries,altogether with interactive sessions.Other classes and online courses are also included for the club members

Project Works

Astronomical Society promotes the brilliance of our engineers to shed new idea-inbuild initiatives,big or small,which may reach out to wide range of poosibilities in space research.We provide with the resources and guidance all the way to your success.

Other Plans

On the due course, the club will be self-sustained to conduct tours and planetorium visits under the mass interest of the club members. The club aims at making a newtonian telescope in our campus.
